Pandemic Simulations

It has been a long time since the first case of COVID-19 (COronaVIrusDisease-2019) was identified. Yet, three waves and many variants later, we still hear things like "masks are ineffective" or "more people die of car accidents than COVID..." Our goal for this website is to help you better understand the pandemic using SIMULATIONS.

Check out our first simulation:
How Does a Pandemic Develop?
