How Social Distancing Can Help Your Neighborhood Effectively Fight Coronavirus?

On December 26, 2019 four unusual cases of pneumonia was noticed by Dr. Jixian Xhang. On January 7th, Coronavirus (COVID-19) was identified as the cause. Since then, the virus has infected thousands of people all over the world at an exponential rate. 900,000+ cases so far!

Here is why the virus spreads so quicky:

(1) The coronavirus spreads from person-to-person when people are in close contact with one another (within 6 feet).
(2) A person may get coronavirus by touching a surface or object that has the virus on it and then touching their own face.

Both these things happen a lot during a typical day in which people are traveling, shopping, socializing with friends, etc.

An urgent problem is that hospitals are unable to manage this rapid and exponential growth of coronavirus cases. For every 10 people in the neighborhood, there is 1 hospital bed avaiable for care. This means, not everyone who is infected can get the treatment they need! (9 out of 10 people)

You can help slow down the rate at which coronavirus is spreading by practicing Social Distancing.

Our goal is to ensure that the number of patients are always fewer than the number of hospital beds available. This gives hospitals enough resources to take care of all sick patients at any give time. This also gives us enough time to develop vaccines and treatment.

How can you practice social distancing?
(1) Stay put in your home and do not travel for work/school. (2) Avoid large gatherings. (3) Stay away from public places like the beach or park. (4) If you go outside like the grocery store, maintain atleast 6 feet distance from others.
